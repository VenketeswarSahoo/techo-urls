How to Delete Your Techo Account

To request deletion of your Techo account, please follow these steps:

  1. Click on the Profile Icon located at the top-left corner of the Home page.
  2. Navigate to the Delete Account section.
  3. Fill out the Delete Account Form with the required information.
  4. Submit the request.

Once we receive your request, our Admin team will contact you shortly to process your account deletion.
